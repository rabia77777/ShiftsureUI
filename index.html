The Anatomy of a Perfect Match

Follow the journey of a single shift from posting to payment—all in minutes. Scroll to see how it works.

1. Urgent Need Arises

Jane Doe, a manager at Toronto Red Cross, needs to fill a critical Dental Specialist shift. She opens the ShiftSure Employer App.

2. Post in Seconds

She quickly enters the job details and schedule. With a single tap, the job is live and sent to the ShiftSure network.

3. AI Finds the Match

Our AI instantly scans thousands of profiles. A notification is sent to John Doe, a top-rated local specialist whose credentials and availability are a perfect match.

4. One-Tap Application

John reviews the details and competitive pay. He submits his verified resume with a single tap, sending his application back to the employer.

5. Instant Review

Jane is notified and sees John's application. The AI has already provided a 91% resume score, highlighting him as a top candidate.

6. Seamless Interviewing

Impressed, Jane schedules an in-person interview directly through the app. The invite is sent to John instantly.

7. The Hire

After a successful interview, Jane hires John with one click. The official offer and shift booking are confirmed through the platform.

8. Problem Solved

The shift is filled. John sees the confirmed shift appear in his calendar, ready to go. The entire process took just minutes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ShiftSure free for healthcare professionals?

Yes! The ShiftSure app is completely free for all healthcare professionals to download and use. There are no fees for finding or booking shifts.

How are credentials verified?

We use a multi-step, AI-assisted verification process. This includes primary source verification for licenses and certifications to ensure all professionals on our platform are fully compliant and ready to work.

What kind of facilities use ShiftSure?

We partner with a wide range of healthcare facilities, including hospitals, long-term care homes, retirement communities, clinics, and more. This provides a diverse range of shift opportunities for our professionals.

How quickly can I get paid after a shift?

With our instant pay options, you can have your earnings deposited into your account within hours of completing and verifying your timesheet through the app.
